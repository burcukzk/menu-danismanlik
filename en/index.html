About

Kitchen to kitchen, sea to village.

My 20+ year career began in Istanbul at Cibalıkapı Balıkçısı, where I learned the Armenian, Greek and Turkish meze traditions. I joined Mövenpick Hotel Istanbul as an intern and was promoted to Chef de Partie (Garde Manger) after three months, running the cold kitchen at its à la carte restaurant, Azzur. I went on to be executive chef at Fire House, chef de partie at Crowne Plaza Harbiye, and Garde Manger at sea with Celebrity Cruises (Celebrity Infinity).

For 11 years I served as a senior flying chef at DO&CO for Turkish Airlines, preparing business-class menus on flights of over 10 hours. Along the way I cooked as a pastry chef at the Abu Dhabi and Mexico Formula 1 races and at DO&CO Munich. From my base in Ayvalık, I now bring all of that experience to restaurants, hotels and yachts, online and on-site.

FAQ

Questions you might have.

Is the consultancy fully online?
Most of the process works online: calls, menu analysis, recipe cards and cost sheets. For team training and kitchen observation I also do on-site visits along the Aegean coast; travel to other cities is planned separately.
How long does it take?
The Basic package usually completes within a week. Standard takes 3–6 weeks depending on menu size. If you're aiming for season opening, starting early is always an advantage.
Do you help brand-new businesses too?
Yes. I support new openings end-to-end: concept, menu structure, kitchen workflow and pre-opening team training. Just tick "starting from scratch" on the form.
Why do yacht galleys need specific expertise?
Tight space, limited cold storage, cooking in swell and provisioning can't be learned ashore. With my STCW certificate, seaman's book and galley experience at Celebrity Cruises, I build yacht menus around those realities.
Is the first call really free?
Yes, the 30-minute discovery call is completely free with zero commitment. The goal is simply to see if we're a good fit.
